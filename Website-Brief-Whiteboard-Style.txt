LOOK AND FEEL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The entire site should feel like you're looking at a whiteboard in a meeting 
room or workshop. Think: collaborative planning session, brainstorming, 
sketching out ideas together.

Background:
- Off-white / cream colour (not pure white â€” too harsh)
- Subtle texture like whiteboard surface or paper grain
- Optional: very faint grid lines like graph paper or dotted grid

Typography:
- Headlines: Hand-drawn / marker style font (e.g., Permanent Marker, Caveat, 
  Architects Daughter, Patrick Hand, or similar Google Fonts)
- Body text: Clean but casual (e.g., Nunito, Quicksand, or a neat handwriting font)
- Annotations: Scribbly, like someone wrote notes in the margin

Colours â€” WHITEBOARD MARKER PALETTE:
- Primary: Black marker (#2d2d2d or similar â€” not pure black)
- Accent 1: Blue marker (#2563eb or similar dry-erase blue)
- Accent 2: Red marker (#dc2626 or similar)
- Accent 3: Green marker (#16a34a or similar)
- Accent 4: Orange marker (#ea580c) â€” use for highlighting BMAD/new content
- Background: Off-white (#f8f6f1 or #fdfbf7)

Visual Elements:
- Hand-drawn boxes, circles, underlines
- Arrows connecting ideas (sketchy, not perfect)
- Sticky notes for key quotes or callouts
- Doodles and simple icons (stick figures, lightbulbs, checkmarks)
- "Circled" important words
- Crossed-out text for "wrong" approaches
- Stars or asterisks for emphasis

Animations:
- Text/elements should "draw in" like someone is writing on the board
- Lines and arrows animate as if being drawn
- Fade-in with slight hand-wobble effect
- Scrolling reveals content progressively (like moving along a long whiteboard)
