The Planning Spectrum

Not everything needs the same level of planning

Complexity What You Need Example
Simple Good prompt, clear ask "Summarise this email"
Medium Quick plan ‚Äî what goes in, what comes out, what's success "Draft responses to common complaints"
Complex Structured approach, documents, maybe multiple agents "Build a system that handles complaints end-to-end"

The key: Match your planning to your complexity.

Don't over-engineer simple stuff.
Don't under-plan complex stuff.

Where Things Go Wrong

I made all of these mistakes. Learn from my pain!

1. "Just One More Thing" Syndrome

Scope keeps growing. AI loses track. You lose track. Chaos.

2. No Shared Context

AI forgets what you agreed 10 prompts ago. You're constantly re-explaining.

3. Copy-Paste Chaos

Moving code between tools. Losing formatting. Indentation hell.

4. Too Deep to Restart

Invested so much time you can't explore better approaches.

5. No Verification

It looks right... but does it actually work? You can't tell.

Has anyone experienced any of these? Which one hurts most?
